## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is White Borneo kratom good for?

White Borneo is most commonly used for energy, mental focus, and mood elevation. It's a popular daytime strain favored by users who want clean stimulation without the sedation associated with red vein strains.

### Is White Borneo stronger than White Maeng Da?

Generally, White Maeng Da is considered more potent due to its higher alkaloid concentration. White Borneo is often described as smoother and more balanced — making it more accessible for regular use.

### How is White Borneo different from Green Borneo?

White Borneo is harvested earlier, producing a more energizing, stimulating alkaloid profile. Green Borneo is harvested at a middle stage — delivering a balanced mix of energy and mild relaxation. White Borneo is sharper on focus; Green Borneo is gentler overall.

### Can I use White Borneo every day?

Daily use is a personal decision best discussed with a healthcare professional. Many experienced users recommend strain rotation and periodic breaks to manage tolerance.

### How do I verify my White Borneo kratom is authentic?

Request or access the Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from an ISO-accredited third-party lab. Confirm the batch number matches your product. Check that mitragynine percentage, heavy metal results, and microbial results are all included. Jack Botanicals provides this documentation publicly for every batch.

### Is kratom legal in my state?

Kratom is federally legal in the United States but banned or regulated in several states including Alabama, Arkansas, Indiana, Rhode Island, Vermont, and Wisconsin. Always verify your local laws before purchasing. (Source: American Kratom Association, State Legality Map, 2023)
